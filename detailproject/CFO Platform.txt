1. Overview
This document summarizes all required modules for developing the CFO Platform, structured across 3 development phases and aligned with a subscription model sold per company (tenant-level subscription).
Platform Goals
* Provide a full CFO toolset for SMEs and enterprises
* Support manual ? connected ? AI-driven workflows
* Deliver modular design for scalable subscription-based business model
* Ensure each company (tenant) operates in an isolated environment (multi-tenant)
Development Phases
* Phase 1 ñ Manual CFO Platform
Full CFO functions, manual import & calculation
* Phase 2 ñ Connected CFO Platform
Integrations, automation, workflow, billing engine
* Phase 3 ñ AI CFO Platform
Forecasting, anomaly detection, AI scenario engine

3. Phase Mapping Summary

Phase 1 ñ Manual Platform
Included Modules:
* User Management & Lifecycle (Add/Invite/Remove user)
* Subscription & Billing UI (basic)
* Manual Reports
* Manual ETL
* Manual Financial Projection & Scenario
* Basic Workflow (user operations)
* Admin Console: basic user controls
Purpose:
* Provide full CFO functionality with manual data handling
* Establish multi-tenant foundation + subscription structure

Phase 2 ñ Connected Platform
Included Modules:
* ETL Parameter Setup (FX, inflation, credit term, depreciation)
* Billing & Payment System (full integration)
* User Lifecycle Audit Log
* Extended Workflow (expense/budget/payment approvals)
* External Integrations (ERP, e-Tax, Bank, Payment Gateway)
Purpose:
* Automate data flow across systems
* Strengthen subscription billing
* Reduce manual work for accounting/finance

Phase 3 ñ AI CFO Platform
Included Modules:
* AI Document Extraction (PP.30)
* AI Scenario Engine
* AI Forecasting (revenue, cashflow, expense)
* AI Anomaly Detection
* AI Recommendations & Insights
Purpose:
* Add AI intelligence for decision support
* Predict risks and opportunities
* Elevate the platform to a ìVirtual CFO Assistantî
